Volunteer

Give Your Time. Support the Mission.

There is a place for local volunteers, NRI supporters, remote contributors, and group teams inside the mission.

In-person support

Support health camps, school drives, community coordination, and public communication.

Remote

Teaching support, design, public updates, fundraising, and communications.

NRI visit programs

Plan service days during India visits by coordinating with the trust contact in advance.

Group service days

Bring employee, student, or community teams into meaningful volunteering programs.

What volunteers do

Health camp support, teaching, program coordination, public communication, and fundraising support.

Time commitment

Weekend, monthly, project-based, and remote support can be coordinated directly with the trust office.

Volunteer focus areas

Volunteers can support education, healthcare coordination, fundraising, communications, and group service planning.

Remote contributors and NRI supporters can coordinate with the trust office before planning any activity or campaign.

What to share when you contact

Can NRIs volunteer?

Yes. NRI volunteers can support remotely or coordinate service days during India visits by calling the trust office in advance.

Do I need special skills?

No. Medical, teaching, design, language, coordination, and communication skills are useful, but meaningful roles can fit many volunteers.

Options can range from one-time support to recurring monthly coordination.
