Giving From Abroad

Support People Through a Safe Giving Process.

For supporters living outside India, any financial contribution or service coordination needs clarity on compliance, receipts, and trusted use of funds.

Why NRI giving is different

Sending money to family is a remittance. Donating to an Indian nonprofit from abroad must follow applicable foreign contribution rules. The goal is simple: your support reaches people legally, transparently, and with the right receipt trail.

  • Confirm FCRA eligibility before international transfers
  • Use official bank or payment details only
  • Keep donation receipt and passport/PAN details where required
Country Guide

General steps for supporters abroad.

NRI and international supporters should confirm local tax treatment in their country of residence and coordinate transfer details directly with the trust office when financial support is involved.

Verify

Contact the trust office before sending funds so official account details, receipt requirements, and compliance steps are clear.

Transfer

Use only verified bank or payment details shared through the official trust contact channels.

Record

Keep transfer confirmation, donor identity details where required, and the donation receipt for your records.

FCRA compliance

FCRA compliance protects both donor and trust. It ensures foreign contributions enter through the correct registered account and are used for permitted charitable activities.

  • International giving: coordinate directly with G.ABHINETHRA
  • Bank details are confirmed by phone before foreign transfers
  • Donor name, country, phone number, transfer confirmation, and receipt details

International payment methods

NRI FAQ

Questions supporters ask before contributing from abroad.

Can NRIs donate to Indian charities?

Yes, but foreign donations must follow FCRA rules and use approved channels.

Is my donation tax deductible abroad?

Tax deductibility depends on your country and personal tax status. CBN Trust can provide donation receipt support, and donors should confirm local tax treatment with their advisor.

Can I contribute from abroad?

Yes. International donors should coordinate with G.ABHINETHRA and use the verified CBN TRUST bank details before sending funds.

How do I verify CBN Trust is legitimate?

Review registration numbers, receipts, program reports, and direct office details before giving.

Can associations organize group giving?

Yes. Contact CBN Trust for a campaign page, program focus area, and reporting template.

Can I support a specific program area?

Yes. Supporters can discuss education, healthcare, or community development preferences with G.ABHINETHRA before giving.
