Programs That Reach the Last Mile

CBN Trust works across education, healthcare, and community development so families are supported where the gaps are sharpest.

Learning resources, school support, child sponsorship, continuity support, and infrastructure help.

SHGs, women's livelihoods, community infrastructure, water access, and local ownership.

Education Deep-Dive

Keeping children in classrooms.

Education programs focus on the everyday blockers that push children out of school: distance, supplies, infrastructure, mentoring, and family financial pressure.

  • School support and learning centers
  • Child sponsorship and term support
  • Education continuity through community follow-up
Support a Child
Healthcare Deep-Dive

Care that reaches people before crisis.

Families can face real barriers to timely basic screening. CBN Trust health programs can close this gap through camps, mobile outreach, and volunteer-supported drives.

  • Primary screening and care coordination
  • Eye care and health guidance
  • Maternal health guidance and medicine access support
Community Deep-Dive

Development built with communities.

Community development is more than aid. It means SHGs, women's livelihoods, community infrastructure, and support systems that let families stand on their own feet.

  • Women's livelihood and SHG support
  • Community infrastructure and water access coordination
  • Local volunteer coordination
